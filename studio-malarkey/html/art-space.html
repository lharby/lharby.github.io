STUDIO INFORMATION.

The main Malarkey gallery/event space is 64.3m2 immediately accessed from the stairwell. A private kitchen and lavatory is available to the side of the studio.

HIRE RATES

  • Full day (9am - 5pm) £200
  • Half day (9.00am - 1.00pm or 1.00pm to 5.00pm) £100

  • Each additional hour after 5pm £40 per hour

  • We are happy to discuss arrangements for longer periods. Please contact us to register interest.

NOTES

  • The studio is on the third floor of an historic building which unfortunately has only stair access. We urge anyone approaching us with public-facing ideas to consider hybrid in-person/online access.
  • We expect any expressions of interest to include a level of mindfulness and respect for the comfort and health of others. We do not enter the studio if we are ill and we require the same consideration from anyone wishing to visit or work with us.
