Malarkey members

Sam P Gibson is an artist based in Northamptonshire, UK.

An embroiderer for over 45 years, her work is typographical, mixing painting and stitch to recreate snapshots of conversation or thoughts.

Sam likes nothing more than turning a three-second joke into an arduous sewing task that takes months.

Sam also works with miniatures, photography and is interested in creating relics, myths and fake histories. An ongoing drawing and stitched project involves collaborating with every person she meets.

Jessica Harby was born in 1980 in Oak Lawn, Il, USA and studied at The School of the Art Institute of Chicago. Her practice encompasses drawing, installation, video, sound and performance and mixes the delicate, the brutal, the absurd and the serious.

Jessica is co-creator of the zine Tappa Tappa Tappa and one of six artists selected by Contemporary Visual Arts Network East Midlands for their two-year project Document.

Luke Harby is an artist who works solely with photographic film.

Recent work has been shown with CTypeMag, Humble Arts Foundation, Localhost gallery, Light Leaked, Editions l'Heliotrope, Lenscratch, Hand Magazine, Noice Magazine, Streitlab, Float Magazine and Lumen London. He has shown work in the US, Spain, Italy, France, Hungary and more regularly in the UK.

Boo is a performance artist and head of security at Studio Malarkey.

Her practice focuses heavily on walking, chomping, and sniffing.
