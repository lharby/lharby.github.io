STUDIO INFORMATION.

The main Malarkey gallery/event space is 64.37m immediately accessed from the stairwell. A private kitchen and lavatory is available to the side of the studio.

Access notes: The studio is on the third floor of an historic building which unfortunately has only stair access. We urge anyone approaching us with public-facing ideas to consider hybrid in-person/online access.
