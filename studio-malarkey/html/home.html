WELCOME TO STUDIO MALARKEY.

Studio MALARKEY is a new art space in central Northampton. Connected to the working studios of artists Sam Gibson, Jessica Harby and Luke Harby, Studio MALARKEY is now welcoming expressions of interest for short-term projects from creative groups and people.

For more information on Studio MALARKEY, visit our Art space page.

Access notes: Studio MALARKEY is on the third floor of an historic building which unfortunately only has stair access. We urge anyone approaching us with public-facing ideas to consider hybrid in-person/online access.
