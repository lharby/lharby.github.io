WELCOME TO STUDIO MALARKEY.

Studio Malarkey is a working artist studio in central Northampton.

Sam Gibson, Jessica Harby and Luke Harby occupy spaces at Studio Malarkey connected to a large main space, pictured here.

As we consider what we'd like for the future, we're inviting ideas/expressions of interest from creatives for short-term projects in the main space.

Access notes: The studio is on the third floor of a historic building which unfortunately has only stair access. We urge anyone approaching us with public-facing ideas to consider hybrid in-person/online access.
